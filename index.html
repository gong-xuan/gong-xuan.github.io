Bio

I am a Postdoc Fellow in the Department of Biomedical Informatics at Harvard Medical School, advised by Prof. Kun-Hsing Yu. I received my bachelor's and master's degrees from Beihang University, and earned my PhD in Computer Science from University at Buffalo.
During my PhD, I worked as a Research Intern at Google VR, Meta Reality Lab, Alibaba DAMO USA, OPPO US Research, and UII America.

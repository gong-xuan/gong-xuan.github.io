Bio

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Yu Lab @ Department of Biomedical Informatics, Harvard Medical School.

I received my bachelor's and master's degrees from Beihang University, and earned my PhD in Computer Science from University at Buffalo. During my PhD, I worked as a Research Intern at Google VR, Meta Reality Lab, Alibaba DAMO USA, OPPO US Research, and UII America.
